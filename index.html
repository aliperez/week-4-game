Welcome to the Amazing Crystals Collector Game!

How to play:


If you can make your score match the target number, you win! But go over, and you lose!

By clicking on each of the four crystals below you will add points to your total score. You won't know how much each crystal is worth until you click on it.

The target number and the value of each crystal will reset at the beginning of a new game.
